History & Heritage

House Worm traces its origins to the fertile soils of the Western Farmlands, where their ancestors developed remarkable abilities to work unseen and transform the world from below. For generations, the Worms have been respected for their humility, diligence, and ability to create foundations upon which others build.

Founded by Lord Terrus the Humble during a time when the kingdom's soil had grown barren, House Worm established itself as essential to the realm's prosperity. Their ancestral estate, Undersoil Manor, is known for its extensive underground chambers and remarkable gardens.

The house colors of brown and red represent their connection to earth and vitality. Their sigil, the industrious worm, symbolizes their philosophy that the most important work often happens out of sight, and that true strength comes from patience and persistence.

The Lord of House Worm

Lord Jacob the Diligent

Eighth Place Contender of MMXXIV

Lord Jacob rose to leadership of House Worm through his exceptional work ethic and humble dedication to improvement. Known for his quiet determination and willingness to tackle the most challenging tasks, he has led his house with dignity in the Banners and Brews tournament of 2024.

His lordship is characterized by steady effort and remarkable humility - qualities that have earned House Worm respect despite their eighth-place finish. Under his guidance, House Worm has become known for their sportsmanship and for laying groundwork for future success.

Lord Jacob's favorite saying: "Today's efforts become tomorrow's foundations."
