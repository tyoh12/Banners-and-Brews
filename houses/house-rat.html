History & Heritage

House Rat emerged from the shadowy alleys of the kingdom's greatest cities, where their ancestors thrived through wit and adaptability. For generations, the Rats have been respected for their resourcefulness and ability to find opportunity where others see only obstacles.

Founded by Lady Whiskers the Shrewd during the kingdom's darkest hour, House Rat quickly proved its worth by navigating political intrigues and surviving against all odds. Their ancestral home, Labyrinth Manor, is a marvel of ingenious design and hidden passages.

The house colors of gray and silver represent their urban origins and their silver achievements. Their sigil, the vigilant rat, symbolizes their intelligence, adaptability, and surprising strength when cornered.

The Lord of House Rat

Lord Chris the Clever

High Contender of MMXXIV

Lord Chris rose to leadership of House Rat through his exceptional strategic thinking and remarkable ability to adapt to changing circumstances. Known for his quick decision-making and resourcefulness, he has led his house to an impressive second place in the Banners and Brews tournament of 2024.

His lordship is characterized by innovative approaches to challenges and finding unconventional solutions - qualities that have earned House Rat the title of High Contender. Under his guidance, House Rat has become known for turning disadvantages into advantages.

Lord Chris's favorite saying: "The cleverest path is rarely the most obvious one."
