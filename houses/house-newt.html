History & Heritage

House Newt traces its lineage to the crystal-clear streams of the Eastern Valleys, where their ancestors developed remarkable adaptability and regenerative abilities. For generations, the Newts have been respected for their ability to recover from setbacks and transform challenges into opportunities.

Founded by Lady Salamandra the Transformative during a time of great change in the kingdom, House Newt established itself as a house that could adapt to any situation. Their ancestral home, Regeneration Keep, stands as a testament to their philosophy of constant renewal and improvement.

The house colors of orange and blue represent their fiery spirit and connection to water. Their sigil, the adaptable newt, symbolizes their philosophy of transformation and renewal in the face of adversity.

The Lord of House Newt

Lord Kyle the Adaptable

Seventh Place Contender of MMXXIV

Lord Kyle rose to leadership of House Newt through his exceptional ability to adapt to changing circumstances and learn from every experience. Known for his innovative approaches and willingness to try new strategies, he has led his house to a respectable seventh place in the Banners and Brews tournament of 2024.

His lordship is characterized by continuous improvement and remarkable adaptability - qualities that have earned House Newt recognition for their creative solutions to challenges. Under his guidance, House Newt has become known for their ability to transform apparent disadvantages into unique strengths.

Lord Kyle's favorite saying: "Every setback contains the seeds of future success."
